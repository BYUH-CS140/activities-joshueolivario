City of Balanga

Just 9 miles north of Orion lies Balanga City, the capital of the province of Bataan and the only city in the province. Despite its status, Balanga offers a peaceful escape from the busier towns, with scenic views and a welcoming atmosphere. The city is known for its beautiful campsites, often used as locations for formal photo shoots. It is also home to one of the oldest cathedrals in the country—a historic landmark that survived the devastation of World War II.
