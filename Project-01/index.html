Behold, Bataan!

Nestled in the heart of Central Luzon, Bataan is a land where history, nature, and culture come alive. From the solemn grounds of Mount Samat to the peaceful shores of its coastal towns, every corner tells a story of resilience and beauty. This photo tour invites you to explore the soul of my hometown—its people, landscapes, and spirit. Come and see Bataan through my eyes, where every image captures a piece of home.

All of the images included are from my Home Town, Orion, Bataan.
