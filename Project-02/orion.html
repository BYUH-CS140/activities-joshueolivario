A Glimpse of Orion, Bataan

I live in a small village called Pag-asa, which means "hope" in English. It is located within the borders of Barangay Wawa—with Wawa meaning "the mouth of the river." Our village is blessed with a river that flows into Manila Bay, making fishing the most common source of livelihood in the area. I grew up in a family of fishermen, and this peaceful riverside community is where I spent my childhood, playing by the water and exploring nature. The images below capture the beauty of the place where I was raised—each photo tells a story of the land that shaped my early years.
